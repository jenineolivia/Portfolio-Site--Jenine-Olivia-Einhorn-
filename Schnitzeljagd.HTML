Schnitzeljagd

WHAT IS IT: Schnitzeljagd is a Scavenger Hunt Application and Scavengers web app to Find Adventure.
COMMISSIONED BY: CareerFoundry Certified UX Designer course project
TIMEFRAME: August 2018 to March 2019 for the MVP development.
MY ROLE: UX Architect, User Researcher, UI Designer
 
PROBLEM: Scavengers need a way todiscover the unknown, locate nearby vistas, and feel supported in a community of other adventure seekers.
PROCESS: Competitive analysis; User research; Persona development; User flows; Card sorts; Rapid prototyping; Usability and Preference testing; Design system development; UI development.
SOLUTION: Architect a custom experience based on profile questions that offers up compatible locations, trails and connect, chat, and meet explorers.
INTENDED OUTCOME: Positive reviews from Enthusiasts who report a bespoke experience and increased confidence in their adventures. Scavnegers report an increase in and better relationships.
CHALLENGES ALONG THE WAY: Finding nature enthusiasts in the beginning. None wanted to participate in interviews and, by extension, usability tests, so online research was done to fill in the gaps about Scavengers and the focus was shifted to the Enthusiastâ€™s user journeys for the rest of the exercise.
FUTURE PLANS: Continue with MVPs for the itinerary creating as well as flesh out the questionnaire. Once complete, use these to get buy-in from nature enthusiasts to further develop their journeys.
LINKS: View the full case study and view the prototype.
CONCLUSION: This is an app that both nature Enthusiasts and Scanvengers can get behind and enjoy using. Come for inspiration. Leave with no regrets.
