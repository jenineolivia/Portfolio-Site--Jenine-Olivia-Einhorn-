LEX

WHAT IS IT: LEX is a Vocabulary Application and Students web app to expand vocabulary.
COMMISSIONED BY: CareerFoundry Certified UX Designer course project
TIMEFRAME: August 2018 to March 2019 for the MVP development.
MY ROLE: UX Architect, User Researcher, UI Designer
 
PROBLEM: All people need a way tolearn everyday, broaden their vocabulary, and feel confident in language and communication with others.
PROCESS: Competitive analysis; User research; Persona development; User flows; Card sorts; Rapid prototyping; Usability and Preference testing; Design system development; UI development.
SOLUTION: Architect a custom experience based on profile questions that offers up compatible exercises, definitions and learn, grow, and speak eloquently.
INTENDED OUTCOME: Positive reviews from Enthusiasts who report a bespoke experience and increased confidence in their vocabulary. Students report an increase in vocabulary and better communication.
CHALLENGES ALONG THE WAY: Finding students in the beginning. None wanted to participate in interviews and, by extension, usability tests, so online research was done to fill in the gaps about students and the focus was shifted to the studentâ€™s user journeys for the rest of the exercise.
FUTURE PLANS: Continue with MVPs for the cirriculum as well as flesh out the questionnaire. Once complete, use these to get buy-in from students to further develop their education.
LINKS: View the full case study.
CONCLUSION: This is an app that both nature Enthusiasts and Scanvengers can get behind and enjoy using. Come for inspiration. Leave with no regrets.
